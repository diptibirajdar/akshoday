Agriclutural Commodities.

Our journey began with a commitment to deliver freshness and goodness straight from the farm to your table. Akshoday believes in the transformative power of organic farming, promoting sustainable practices that not only benefit consumers but also contribute to the well-being of our environment.
Akshay Datir, the visionary behind Akshoday, has instilled a culture of excellence and customer-centricity in our operations. His dedication to quality and a sustainable food ecosystem has driven our expansion, making Akshoday a household name. Currently, we proudly operate in 15 locations in Pune and have expanded our reach to 3 places in Mumbai, bringing the farm-fresh experience closer to urban dwellers.
What sets Akshoday apart is not just the variety and quality of our farm products but also our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We believe that everyone deserves access to nutritious, organic produce, and we work tirelessly to make this vision a reality.
Join us on this flavorful journey with Akshoday, where every vegetable tells a story of care, quality, and a commitment to a greener, healthier tomorrow. Explore our markets, taste the freshness, and be a part of our growing community that values the goodness of nature in every bite.
